About placement cell

The placement cell is designed to function with a high degree of professionalism. The placement cell manages its activities by the support of staff coordinators and the effort of students and all Head of Departments. The cell provides almost instantaneous data to the corporate sector about the candidates available for consideration towards placement under the prescribed requirements.

placementcell activities

Our placement cell always works with a dedicated work attitude to accomplish the mission of 100% placement. To fulfill the responsibility of Training and Placement Cell, they arrange and coordinate various programmes that will mould our students to meet the industry expectations in career building and turn to bring laurels to the parent institution. Training and Placement cell takes care of honing the minds of our students to face the competitive world with courage, confidence, and wisdom.

placementcell activities includes

Organizing and coordinates the Campus Placement Program, to fulfill its commitment of a job to every aspirant. Organizing training sessions aiming at making the student community employable. Nurturing a cordial relationship with the Industries, invite them for Campus recruitment drives, organizing technical seminars, workshops, and other technical sessions. Industry personnel are invited periodically to enrich the knowledge base of our student community with the latest technological innovations and industry practices. Promotes career counseling through guidance lectures by suitable corporate representatives. Maintaining an updated database and job profile, recruitment pattern of the companies and thus helps each student analyze and prepare their mindset. The department has in its active file database of voluminous records of corporates. Helps every student define his/her career interest through individual expert counseling
